Q: What are greenz?
A: "Greenz" is a digital currency for making social payments. Every unit is called a "green."  
Q: What are social payments and why should I use greenz?
A: Social payments are the way you express appreciation for things that your friends create or you find on the web. You can read more about social payments on wikipedia.
Q: How do I get and use greenz?
A: Greenz are digital money you can hold and spend through our wallet app (for iOS, Android or the chrome browser). When you first sign up, we will give you 200 greenz as a gift. To get more greenz, you can use our "tipping" system or exchange bitcoins directly for greenz (using Coinbase).
Q: Are greenz real money?
A: Yep. Greenz in your wallet can be spent on the web with just one click. Check out our video to get started.
Q: What are greenz worth?
A: Greenz have a fixed exchange rate to all major currencies. Right now that's about a fifth of a cent per greenz or, to be exact, (1 Green = USD$0.0022).
Q: Are greenz safe to use?
A: Absolutely. Greenz are securely stored in your encrypted wallet app, which is accessible only to you, or in your secure account online with us. When you spend or withdraw greenz, you enter your PIN (or, for large amounts, a special code we send to you via SMS) and we safely process the transaction for you.
Q: Who handles my greenz?
A: You have complete control of your greenz (inside your wallet app or in your online account). When you spend or withdraw greenz, we process the transaction for you.
Q: How do I manage my account?
A: You can manage your account with through our website, via our wallet app (iOS and mobile web ), or through our chrome extension. If you have a balance of $5 or more (US dollars), you can cash out any time. We deliver your money to you through our Coinbase account.
Q: Where is the money?
A: We manage funds carefully so that your greenz always have a stable value. We keep both physical and digital cash onhand to handle payments (80% US dollars and 20% Bitcoins). We are continually working to diversify and protect you from all risks.
Q: Do you charge fees?
A: Only when you exchange out of Greenz back to Bitcoin or regional currencies. If you exchange back to Bitcoin, the fee is 1.5%. If you exchange back to USD, the fee is 2.5%. Contact us about other currencies.
Q: Where can I get more information?
A: There are much longer answers on our Quora channel.
