Q: What is TER?
A: TER is the first real asset-backed token. In particular, it is backed by land. An Estonian legal entity manages the land purchase through nested sub-entities.
Q: Why would I want a land-backed currency?
A: A land backed currency is the ultimate stable unit of account. Our token is designed to be deflationary at first with benefits for early adopters that creates a stable global unit of account.
Q: Where have you identified real estate opportunities?
A: We have three major categories at launch: (1) contracts to develop land through a partnership with the Brazilian government (2) Distressed real assets identified by machine learning algorithms through a partner fund (3) Existing “holons” setup through Ethereum network participants, notably the Crypto-Castle and Love Nest in the Bay Area.
Q: What legal structure are you using?
A: Our legal structure has been derived from swarm.fund and makes use of nested Estonian special purpose vehicles (SPVs). These structures allow legal ownership of land and other assets, as well as back end management for the structure via the smart contracts.
Q: Why is the TER sale uncapped?
A: We plan to acquire a lot of land. Additionally, our discovery process for land purchase is highly automated via machine learning and we have $121mm worth of active deals in our pipeline through partner funds. If we go over that amount we will work out more arrangements with other partner funds.
Q: Will TER appreciate in value?
A: TER’s value is the value of the underlying real estate, any contractual relationships for development on top of that real estate, and the additional use value of a fungible unit of account. All of these have their own value associated with them and may appreciate over time, like any other commodity or token with use-value.
Q: What technology has been devleoped to support this initiative?
A: ibreathe.io has a demo of our smart contract liquid democracy system with the specific purpose of land management. A more fully ufnctional and generic version exists at liquid.qhoss.com. Additionally, there are several javascript widget and a mobile app that were developed in 2013 that can be used to transact micropayments in this currency.
Q: What is TER’s cost structure?
A: TER operates as a standard 2 and 20 fund. This means that 2% goes into operational costs each year and 20% of profit is returned to the fund managers. The other 80% will be redeployed into new real estate opportunities or used to buy up TER tokens on the open market. Additionally, our operating agreement with Swarm.fund has a 1 and 10 agreement, which goes to the maintainance of our SPVs and reporting infrastructure.
Q: What is TEC?
A: TEC is the alternative ETC implementation of the same which can be participated in under the same terms.
Q: Where is the money?
A: We manage funds carefully so that your greenz always have a stable value. We keep both physical and digital cash onhand to handle payments (80% US dollars and 20% Bitcoins). We are continually working to diversify and protect you from all risks.
Q: What is the legal structure?
A: This first global cooperative fund with nested special purpose vehicles in Estonia and potentially other legal structures. Additionally we have contracts with the Brazilian government.
Q: Who is behind Terreth?
A: Our major partners are Swarm.fund, Moeda, Mooti, AgroNow, and Infinity.
